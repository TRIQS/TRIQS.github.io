inchworm
*********

.. sidebar:: inchworm |PROJECT_VERSION|

   This is the homepage of inchworm |PROJECT_VERSION|.
   For changes see the :ref:`changelog page <changelog>`.
      
      .. image:: _static/logo_github.png
         :width: 75%
         :align: center
         :target: https://github.com/triqs/inchworm


inchworm is a TRIQS-based quantum impurity solver. It implements an equilibrium,
imaginary-time Inchworm quantum Monte Carlo solver for quantum impurity models,
based on a continuous-time hybridization expansion and built on the
:ref:`TRIQS <triqslibs:welcome>` library.

This documentation is generated based on `rst <https://de.wikipedia.org/wiki/ReStructuredText>`_ files
and the comments in the sources and headers.

Learn how to use inchworm in the :ref:`documentation`.
