tau_t¶

A point in imaginary time, i.e. $tau in [0,beta]$, but defined on a very fine grid.

The position in the segment is given by an uint32_t, i.e. a very long integer. This allows exact comparisons, which notoriously dangerous on floating point number.

Synopsis:
