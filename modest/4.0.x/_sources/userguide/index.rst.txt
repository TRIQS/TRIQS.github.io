.. _userguide:

User guide
**********

This guide describes the ideas behind ModEST and how they fit together:
realistic DMFT in connection to electronic structure, the role of one-body
elements and embedding, and the interface to the impurity solver. The
theory and notation section collects the conventions used throughout the
rest of the documentation.
