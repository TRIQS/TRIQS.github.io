Detailed Description

The embedding class.

The embeddding class encodes the mapping between impurities and the correlated space ùìí (cf. ModEST implementation notes). In particular, it maps the individual "blocks" within an impurity self-energy to a block and spin of the embedded self-energy function, which can then be upfolded (downfolded) to obtain the lattice self-energy and the local Green's function.
