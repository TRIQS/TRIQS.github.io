Detailed Description

Utility functions for creating the four-index Coulomb tensor. We typically construct the four-index Coulomb tensor in the basis of spherical harmonics, $$ U_{m_{1}m_{2}m_{3}m_{4}}^{\mathrm{spherical}} = \sum_{k=0}^{2l} F_{k} \alpha (l, k, m_{1}, m_{2}, m_{3}, m_{4}),$$ where \(F_{k}\) are radial Slater integrals and \(\alpha(l, k, m_{1}, m_{2}, m_{3}, m_{4})\) denote angular Racah-Wigner numbers for a spherically symmetric interaction tensor.
