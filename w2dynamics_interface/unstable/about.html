Citations¶

If you use w2dynamics_interface in published work, please cite the following papers: [1] for the main code, and [2] for an algorithm for the efficient calculation of the local fermionic trace in cthyb.

1

M. Wallerberger, A. Hausoel, P. Gunacker, A. Kowalski, N. Paragh, F. Goth, K. Held, and G. Sangiovanni, Comput. Phys. Commun. 235, 388 (2019)

2

A. Kowalski, A. Hausoel, M. Wallerberger, P. Gunacker, G. Sangiovanni, arxiv:1807.00361 (2019)
