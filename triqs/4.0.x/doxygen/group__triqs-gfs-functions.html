Detailed Description

Physical operations acting on Green's functions.

This is a parent group for operations frequently applied to Green's functions: computing the particle density (GF density), fitting and manipulating high-frequency tails (GF tail fitting), checking reality and hermiticity (GF reality and hermiticity) and converting between basis representations (GF basis conversions).
