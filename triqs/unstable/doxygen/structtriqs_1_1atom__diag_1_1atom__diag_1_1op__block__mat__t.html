Detailed Description

template<bool Complex>
struct triqs::atom_diag::atom_diag< Complex >::op_block_mat_t

Block-matrix representation of an operator that respects the block structure of the Hamiltonian.

Stores, for every invariant subspace \( B \), the target subspace \( B' \) (or \( -1 \) if the operator annihilates \( B \)) together with the dense matrix block describing the action \( B \to B' \) in the Hamiltonian eigenbasis:

\[ \hat O\, P_B = P_{B'}\, \hat O\, P_B \; , \]

where \( P_B \) projects onto subspace \( B \).
