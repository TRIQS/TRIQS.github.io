Detailed Description

template<typename HilbertSpace, typename ScalarType>
class triqs::hilbert_space::state< HilbertSpace, ScalarType, false >

Many-body state based on nda::vector.

A general many-body state \( \lvert \psi \rangle \) in a Hilbert (Fock) space \( \mathcal{F}^{(m)} \) can be written as a linear combination over all occupation number basis states \( \lvert f_i \rangle \in \mathcal{F}^{(m)} \), i.e.

\[ \lvert \psi \rangle = \sum_{f_i \in \mathcal{F}^{(m)}} a_{f_i} \lvert f_i \rangle \; . \]

Here, \( a_{f_i} \) are the expansion amplitudes/coefficients corresponding to each basis state \( \lvert f_i \rangle \).

The amplitudes \( a_{f_i} \) are stored in an nda::vector \( \mathbf{a} \in \mathbb{R}^M \) or \( \mathbf{a} \in \mathbb{C}^M \), with \( M = \dim(\mathcal{F}^{(m)}) \).

It inherits from boost::additive and boost::multiplicative to provide basic vector space operations.

Calculate the dot product of two states \( \langle \phi | \psi \rangle \).

It computes the dot product as

\[ \langle \phi | \psi \rangle = \sum_{f_i \in \mathcal{F}^{(m)}} b_{f_i}^* a_{f_i} \; , \]

where \( b_{f_i} \) and \( a_{f_i} \) are the amplitudes of the states \( \lvert \phi \rangle \) and \(\lvert \psi \rangle \), respectively.
