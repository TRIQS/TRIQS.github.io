basic_array_view & nda::basic_array_view< ValueType, Rank, Layout, Algebra, AccessorPolicy, OwningPolicy >::operator= ( RHS const &  rhs)
inlinenoexcept

Copies the content of rhs into the view. Pseudo code : for all i,j,k,l,... : this[i,j,k,l,...] = rhs(i,j,k,l,...)

The dimension of RHS must be large enough or behaviour is undefined.

If NDA_BOUNDCHECK is defined, the bounds are checked.
