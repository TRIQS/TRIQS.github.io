gw_sigma_wk_serial_fft¶

Synopsis:

 triqs_tprf::g_wk_t gw_sigma_wk_serial_fft (triqs_tprf::chi_wk_cvt Wr_wk,
triqs_tprf::g_wk_cvt g_wk)

GW self energy \(\Sigma(i\omega_n, \mathbf{k})\) calculator

Parameters¶

  • V_k: static bare interaction \(V_{abcd}(\mathbf{k})\)
  • Wr_wk: retarded screened interaction \(W^{(r)}_{abcd}(i\omega_n, \mathbf{k})\)
  • g_wk: single particle Green’s function \(G_{ab}(i\omega_n, \mathbf{k})\)

Return value¶

GW self-energy \(\Sigma_{ab}(i\omega_n, \mathbf{k})\)

Documentation¶

Fourier transforms the screened interaction and the single-particle Green’s function to imagiary time and real space.

(1)¶\[G_{ab}(\tau, \mathbf{r}) = \mathcal{F}^{-1} \left\{ G_{ab}(i\omega_n, \mathbf{k}) \right\}\]
(2)¶\[W^{(r)}_{abcd}(\tau, \mathbf{r}) = \mathcal{F}^{-1} \left\{ W^{(r)}_{abcd}(i\omega_n, \mathbf{k}) \right\}\]

computes the GW self-energy as the product

(3)¶\[\Sigma_{ab}(\tau, \mathbf{r}) = \sum_{cd} W^{(r)}_{abcd}(\tau, \mathbf{r}) G_{cd}(\tau, \mathbf{r})\]

and transforms back to frequency and momentum

(4)¶\[\Sigma_{ab}(i\omega_n, \mathbf{k}) = \mathcal{F} \left\{ \Sigma_{ab}(\tau, \mathbf{r}) \right\}\]
